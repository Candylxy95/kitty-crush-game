Cat Blocks

How to play?

  • Double Click on connecting cats with the same color to earn points!
  • 2 Cats = 1 Point
  • 3 Cats and above = 2 Points
  • 5 Cats and above = 3 Points
  • 8 Cats and above = 5 Points
  • Accumulate at least 200 Points within a min to win!
